ABI / Calldata

Encode calldata & event topics, decode calldata and logs — from a signature or ABI

Definition
Describe the contract — a single signature, or an ABI. Its functions and events appear below.
A plain signature is listed as both a function and an event; indexed makes it event-only. Examples: balanceOf(address) · Transfer(address indexed from, address indexed to, uint256 value)
