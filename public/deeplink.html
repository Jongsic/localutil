Gotchas to keep in mind
  • Two apps sharing one scheme. If a device has two builds registering the same custom scheme (e.g. prod and QA both use myapp://), a scheme:// link is ambiguous — iOS picks one arbitrarily and Android shows a chooser. Tell the environments apart with the universal / app link instead (distinct hosts), which is unambiguous.
  • iOS: tapping a universal link inside a browser won’t open the app. Apple suppresses universal links for in-page taps in Safari/Chrome (and same-domain navigations never hand off). To open the app: scan the QR with the Camera, tap the link from another app (Notes/Messages/Mail), or long-press it and choose “Open in …”. A scheme:// link does open from the address bar.
