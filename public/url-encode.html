URL Encode / Decode

Percent-encode & decode URLs and components

Full URL encodeURI / decodeURI — keeps : / ? & # intact. For a whole address.
Component encodeURIComponent — escapes everything incl. : / ? & #. Space is %20, + stays +.
Form / Query x-www-form-urlencoded — space becomes + and + decodes to space, like browser query strings.
